Portfolios

Nothing's Impossible Series: This acrylic series was based off the music video for "Nothing's Impossible" by Depeche Mode. It is the first painting series I have done and my most recent work.
Paintings: These are paintings done using several different mediums. I mostly work with acrylics, but have done several in oil and watercolor. This body of work includes paintings from high school through post college.
Design Work: This body of work consists of pieces that focus on design. Medium ranges from watercolor to construction paper to guache to xerox paper to markers.
Drawings: Many of these were quick studies, but this body of work includes multi media projects and larger pieces as well.
Photography: I took several photography classes in high school andblack and white was by far my favorite class. I learned to roll my own film, develop them in the darkroom and make edits.
Publication Design: In high school, I was a designer, photographer and section editor for our yearbook. In college, I was editor-in-chief for two years for our school's publication. Here are some spreads from Stern's Violet Publications.
Crafts: I have always enjoyed making things. More recently, I've begun taking on larger needlework projects.
